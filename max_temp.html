Max Temperature

This graph of max temperature vs lattitude shows that as you get closer to the equator, which is marked by 0 on the graph, cities get warmer. This was no surprise as it is general knowledge that cities are much warmer near the equator. The major takeaway is that it appears the southern hemiphere is much warmer than the northern. This is most likely due to each experiencing inverse seasons.
